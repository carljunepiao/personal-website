Why BisdaC?

The term "BisdaC" is a combination of two words "Bisdak" and "Code". I've come up with the idea to describe myself and that's the result. Bisdak derives from the expression "Bisayang Dakû". Dakû means "big" or "large. The expression means "Visayan to a LARGE degree." So when I say I'm Bisdak, it means "I'm very Visayan." It's an indicator of "how Visayan" a Visayan person is. The letter in C in "BisdaC" means Code which I'd want to emphasize my knowledge in coding and how big it plays a role in my life. I like to code in my free time. When I was a kid I really don't know what coding is and how it works but now I still don't know how it works lol, just joking, now I'm a little bit knowledgeable but I still want to improve my knowledge and master it. In other words:

I'M A CERTIFIED BISAYANG DA_CODE' !
